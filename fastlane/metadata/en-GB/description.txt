Simple Voice Recorder is an easy-to-use voice recording app that starts with just one tap. Supports high-quality audio, background recording, and a range of playback features.

Recording formats: lossless PCM (WAV) and compressed AAC (M4A).
Sample rate: 8kHz–192kHz. Bit depth: 8/16/24/32-bit.
